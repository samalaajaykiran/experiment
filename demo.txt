A farmer once had a magical goose. 
Every day that goose would lay one golden egg.
He used to sell that golden egg in the market.
A farmer once had a magical goose. Every day that goose would lay one golden egg.
But one day, he thought there must be more.
So the next day, he took a knife and cut its belly. 
But there was no egg.


Moral: Greed can lead to great loss.
